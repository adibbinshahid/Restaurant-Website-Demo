Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our pizzas, delivery, orders, and more.

Ordering

Simply browse our menu, select your pizzas and preferred size (S, M, or L), add them to your cart, and click "Place Order." You'll receive an order confirmation with a tracking code.

Each pizza is crafted to a specific recipe to guarantee the best flavour. For special requests (allergies, ingredient removals), please leave a note in the order comments field or call us directly at +01 23 45 67 89.

We offer three sizes for all our pizzas:

  • Small (S) — ideal for 1 person
  • Medium (M) — our most popular, great for 1–2 people
  • Large (L) — perfect for sharing or a very hungry person

There is no minimum order amount. However, orders under $25 are subject to a $2.99 delivery fee. Orders of $25 or more are delivered free of charge.

At checkout, enter your coupon code in the "Promo Code" field and click "Apply." The discount will be calculated and shown in your order summary before payment.

Delivery

We guarantee delivery within 30 minutes from order confirmation. During busy periods (Friday/Saturday evenings) this may extend to 45 minutes. You can track your order live using your order code on the Track Order page.

We currently deliver to 9 zones across Paris and inner suburbs:

  • 75001 — 1er Arrondissement
  • 75008 — 8ème Arrondissement

  • 75015 — 15ème Arrondissement
  • 92100 — Boulogne-Billancourt
  • 92130 — Issy-les-Moulineaux
  • 93100 — Montreuil
  • 94300 — Vincennes

Not in your area? Contact us — we're always looking to expand!

Once your order is confirmed, you'll receive an order code (e.g. LC-0046). Go to our Track Order page, enter your code, and see your real-time delivery status.

We take our 30-minute guarantee seriously. If your delivery arrives significantly late, please contact us at contact@lcinco-pizza.fr or call +01 23 45 67 89, and we will make it right with a discount on your next order.

Payment & Cancellations

We accept all major payment methods:

  • Credit & Debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
  • PayPal
  • Cash on delivery
  • Apple Pay / Google Pay

Orders can be cancelled or modified within 5 minutes of being placed, before they enter our kitchen. After that, the pizza is already being made and we cannot cancel. Please call us immediately at +01 23 45 67 89 if you need to make a change.

If your order arrives incorrect, damaged, or cold, please contact us within 30 minutes of delivery. We will arrange a replacement or full refund — no questions asked. Your satisfaction is our priority.
